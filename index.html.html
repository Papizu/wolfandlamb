Our Legacy

A Midtown Institution


Wolf & Lamb Steakhouse has been a staple in the heart of Midtown Manhattan for over 25 years. Business leaders, celebrities, and political figures have all passed through our doors to experience our old-world cuisine in a fresh, welcoming atmosphere.

We were instrumental at the forefront of a revolution of fine kosher dining — and we remain pioneers in handcrafted, delicious kosher cuisine. Every steak is hand-selected, butchered on premises, and prepared to perfection.

Located steps from Rockefeller Center, the Diamond District, and the Waldorf Astoria — ideal for business meetings, corporate events, and celebrations of all kinds.
